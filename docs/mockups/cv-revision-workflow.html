What should this revision optimize for?

The discovery step should feel like a focused editorial kickoff, not a generic chat. The AI asks a small set of high-signal questions and turns the answers into a structured direction summary.

AI assistant

Before I revise the CV, I want to align on direction. What role are you targeting, what tone should the CV have, and which strengths do you want me to emphasize?

User

Targeting senior staff/backend platform roles. I want it sharper and more technical, less generic consultant language, and more emphasis on architecture, ownership, and mentoring.

AI assistant

Good. What should I downplay? For example: broad lists of responsibilities, older frontend-heavy work, or anything that makes the profile feel less focused?
